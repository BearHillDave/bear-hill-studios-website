About Laura

Voice is a discipline.

Laura trained at the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School — one of the most rigorous actor training programmes in the UK. That foundation shows in every recording: the voice is not performed over, it's inhabited.

Alongside her voice work, Laura is a speech and language therapist specialising in voice therapy. She understands the instrument — technically, physically, clinically. It's a differentiating detail that explains her precision and longevity.
