Laura Cox

Voice Coaching

Combining professional acting experience with clinical Speech and Language Therapy — a uniquely informed approach to vocal development.

My Approach

Where performance meets clinical expertise

I trained as an actor at the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School and spent over ten years working professionally in the industry. Five years ago, I retrained as a Speech and Language Therapist at City, University of London, deepening my understanding of the voice from a clinical and technical perspective.

Alongside this, I co-founded Bear Hill Studios with my husband, creating immersive audiobooks for children, and I continue to work professionally as a voice artist from our home studio in Bath.

My combined experience as a professional voice user and a Speech and Language Therapist allows me to support performers with both vocal technique and vocal hygiene. I understand the demands placed on the voice in performance and can help you develop sustainable techniques to improve your voice, speech, and confidence.

What I offer

Vocal Technique

Breath support, resonance, projection and range — building on solid foundations.

Vocal Hygiene

Practical guidance on protecting your voice for sustainable long-term use.

Speech & Clarity

Articulation, diction and accent work tailored to your goals.

Performance Confidence

Finding and owning your voice for auditions, presentations and broadcasts.
