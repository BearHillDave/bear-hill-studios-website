Voice Artist & Coach

Laura Cox

Warm, natural British voice · Speech & Language Therapist

Laura trained at the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School and worked with Nick Park on Chicken Run before building a career in voice acting spanning the London Olympics, BBC wildlife documentaries, and radio. She later retrained as a Speech and Language Therapist, bringing a rare clinical depth to her voice coaching.

Sound & Interaction Design

Dave Cox

Creative Director · 30 years in digital & interactive

Dave co-founded award-winning digital agency Lean Mean Fighting Machine (later acquired by M&C Saatchi) and spent years leading product design in Virtual Reality. His expertise spans audio, animation, UX and interaction — all guided by a conviction that immersion is the most powerful storytelling tool we have.
