About Laura

A voice built on experience

Laura began her career training at the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School, working with Nick Park and Peter Lord on Chicken Run — voicing additional chicken characters and recording guide tracks for Julia Sawalha and Jane Horrocks.

Her subsequent career spanned the original London Olympic cable car announcements, wildlife documentaries for BBC Studios Natural History, BBC Radio 4 plays, and a wide range of radio ads and narration.

Today, Laura balances voice acting with a clinical practice as a Speech and Language Therapist specialising in voice therapy — a unique combination that makes her an exceptional coach as well as performer.
